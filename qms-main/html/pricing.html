Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about QuizPro pricing and plans

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all our paid plans. No credit card is required to start your trial. You'll have full access to all features of the plan you choose during the trial period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise plans we also support bank transfers and purchase orders.

Do you offer discounts for educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for accredited educational institutions. Please contact our sales team with your institution details to learn more about our education discounts.
